Why we are awesome

AntAmbush is a labor of love, born from a burning desire to toss wild animals at our friends and loved ones. After realizing that this action in reality may cause legal troubles, we realized that throwing digital animals at your friends is not against the law (yet). We spent days in the lab, pouring over textbooks and academic papers, trying to determine the best way to chuck animals at people digitally. What you see before you is the final product. I hope you love it as much as we do.

One day, when the government stops regulating what live objects we can throw at people, this app may just become reality. Until that day comes, you can console yourself by throwing monkeys, puppies, and kittens at your friends through this app. God bless America.
